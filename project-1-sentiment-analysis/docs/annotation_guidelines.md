# Annotation Guidelines

## Task
Label each product review as either Positive or Negative based on customer sentiment.

---

## Positive
Label as Positive if:
- Satisfaction is expressed
- The product meets expectations
- The user is happy or satisfied
- Recommendation is implied

---

## Negative
Label as Negative if:
- Dissatisfaction is expressed
- Expectations are not met
- Complaint or regret appears
- Frustration or disappointment exists

---

## Key Rule
If unclear, decide based on whether the sentence leans toward satisfaction or dissatisfaction.

---

## Notes
Consistency is more important than perfection. Similar sentences should be labeled the same way across the dataset.
